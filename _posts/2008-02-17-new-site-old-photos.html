hurray! i've finally found some evening time to create a flex/flash9 based image viewer for my old (+new) black and white +some colour photos. they can be found at this site's index page. most of the photos are scans of prints i made from the years 98 and on. some are new [digital - from my new nikon]. i'll hopefully be developing the flash viewer to allowing indexing of individual photos later.
with this new development we poignantly went to the ansel adams exhibit at the city art gallery. worth a visit to see prints such as Moonrise, Hernandez, New Mexico 1941, which was taken in a single guessed exposure before the light was lost. inspiring.
