How to Update the Titles/SKUs of Magento Custom Options

This is easy-peasy but someone might find it useful. ...& it's here now in case I forget.

update catalog_product_option_type_value set sku = 'New SKU' where sku = 'Old SKU';
update catalog_product_option_type_title set title = 'New Title' where title = 'Old Title';
