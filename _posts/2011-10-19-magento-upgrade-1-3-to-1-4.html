--- layout: post title: Magento upgrade 1.3 to 1.4 tags: - magento status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: "14182377" jabber_published: "1319037466" --- Yeah, I know Magento 1.4 came out ages ago but Inigo recently had a client ask for an upgrade of their Magento 1.3 site. After upgrading with: 
./pear install magento-core/Mage_All_Latest
./pear upgrade-all

...here's the mods I had to do to get it running (there are still some theme tweaks to do):
  • Remove the addWishlistLink() method 
    app/design/frontend/default/whywoodworks/layout/wishlist.xml
    - <action method="addWishlistLink"></action> + <!--action method="addWishlistLink"></action-->
  • Add a name to the core/profiler block 
    app/design/frontend/default/whywoodworks/layout/page.xml
    - <block type="core/profiler" output="toHtml"/> + <block type="core/profiler" output="toHtml" name="core_profiler"/>
  • removed app/code/core/Zend/Cache/
