Here's a follow up to Sputnik on a Mac

How to run under cgi-bin so you can visit your new Sputnik install here:
http://localhost/cgi-bin/sputnik.cgi:

cd ~/sputnik
cp ./lib/luarocks/bin/wsapi.cgi  ./bin/
cp sputnik.cgi /Library/WebServer/CGI-Executables/
chmod a+x /Library/WebServer/CGI-Executables/sputnik.cgi

...and here's how to add the Tickets App:

cd ~/sputnik
./bin/luarocks \
  --only-from=http://sputnik.freewisdom.org/rocks/earth \
  install sputnik-tickets

You can visit your new Tickets App here:
http://localhost/cgi-bin/sputnik.cgi?p=Tickets
